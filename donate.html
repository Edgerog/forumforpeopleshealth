Solidarity With Women Suffering From Anaemia

One in every two women in India suffer from Anaemia. This International Women's Day, let us take a pledge and stand together to eliminate this major public health crisis.

With 600 rupees, we can support one woman with anaemia tests and the required supplementary medicines for three months to fight anaemia.

So, on this occasion of International Women's Day, 8th


"I pledge to support a woman overcome anaemia by donating a minimum of Rs.600 for her tests and medication!"
